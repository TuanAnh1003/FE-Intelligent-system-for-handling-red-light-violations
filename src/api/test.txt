Kon'nichiwa, watashi wa bui to~uan Aindesu. Danan kōka daigaku no IT gakubu no 3-nenseidesu. Genzai, sofutō~ea kaihatsu-sha to shite no shokumu o tantō shite imasu. Gakkōde wa, omoni senmon chishiki ya C/ C ++, jawa, C# nado no puroguramingu gengo ni tsuite manande imasu. Shikashi, watashi wa u~ebu kaihatsu no bun'ya ni hayai jiki kara jōnetsu o motte ita tame, jiko gakushū de u~ebupuroguramingu ni tsuite mo manabimashita. Daigaku purojekuto ya kojin purojekutode wa, C ++, C#, HTML, CSS, Javascript, ēesupī. NET koa, NuxtJs, VueJs, SQL nado, iroiro ina gengo ya furēmuwāku o shiyō shite kimashita. Shōrai-teki ni wa, furusutakku kaihatsu-sha matawa sofutō~ea enjinia ni naru koto o mezashite imasu. Genzai, furontoendo to bakku endo no ryōhō no puroguramingu ga dekimasu. Korekara, sukiru appu no tame, DevOps ni tsuite mo manabou to omotte imasu. Gakugyō igaide wa, SGroup ya BKStars nado no kurabu ya hieiridantai ni sanka shite imasu. Mata, hakkason ya san* Be the change nado no puroguramingukontesuto ni mo sanka shite imasu. Korera no kontesuto ya soshiki e no sanka o tsūjite, kichōna chishiki to keiken o manabi, seichō suru koto ga dekimashita. Shōrai-teki ni wa atarashī kankyō de jibun jishin ni chōsen shi, seichō suru kikai o etai to omotte imasu. Dōzoyoroshikuonegaishimasu.
